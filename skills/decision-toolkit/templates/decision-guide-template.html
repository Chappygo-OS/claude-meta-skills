Lens A · Step 3 of 9
Start-Fresh Test
If you were starting from zero today with current knowledge, would you choose this path? This strips sunk costs and legacy assumptions.
Lens B · Step 4 of 9
Stakeholder Mapping
Every decision affects people beyond the decision-maker. Map them before committing.
Lens C · Step 5 of 9
Bias Audit
Check which cognitive biases may be distorting your judgment. Check all that apply.
Lens D · Step 6 of 9
Timing Test
The right decision at the wrong time is still wrong. Is this a two-way door (reversible) or a one-way door (irreversible)?
Lens E · Step 7 of 9
Opportunity Cost
Every choice forecloses other choices. Make the true cost visible.
Lens F · Step 8 of 9
Pre-mortem
Travel 12 months into the future. The decision was made — and it failed spectacularly. What happened?
Lens G + H · Step 9 of 9
Scenarios + Regret Test
